Customer Segmentation Dashboard

📌 Overview
This repository contains a dataset of customer feedback and behavioral metrics, along with scripts and visualizations for segmentation analysis. The goal is to uncover actionable insights that help businesses understand their customers, identify key trends, and make better strategic decisions.

📂 Contents
Dataset: Raw customer feedback and behavioral data.
Data Preparation: Cleaning and preprocessing scripts.
Segmentation Analysis: Clustering and grouping logic.
Dashboard: Interactive visualizations for decision-making.
Documentation: Guides and notes on methodology.

🔍 Insights
The dashboard highlights:
Segment Distribution → Proportion of customer groups.
Revenue Contribution → Which segments drive the most income.
Engagement Trends → How customer activity changes over time.
Satisfaction Scores → Feedback patterns across demographics.
Churn Risk → Early signals of declining engagement.

📊 Dashboard Features
Interactive Filters: Drill down by demographics, time, or region.
Visual Storytelling: Clear charts (pie, bar, line, heatmap, scatter).
Decision Support: KPIs and annotations highlight what matters most.
Usability: Simple layout, consistent colors, and intuitive navigation.

🎯 Purpose
This project emphasizes clarity, usability, and impact — ensuring that insights are not just displayed but communicated in a way that drives better business decisions.
